## Requiring a customized support

If you weren't able to find the solution you were looking for or if your issue isn't listed within this site, Bloxproducts offer a customized support for customers through tickets which can be created through their [Discord server](https://discord.gg/bloxproducts).

## Guidelines

<Warning>
  There are requirements that you must follow before creating a ticket. If your ticket is to be seen without the mention requirements, it will automatically be closed.
</Warning>
